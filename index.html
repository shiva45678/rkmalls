Our Story

Proudly serving Nandigama's fashion needs.

Serving Nandigama since 2017 with quality fashion.

RK Family Shopping Mall is Nandigama's one-stop destination for readymade garments. Our mission is to provide stylish, affordable clothing with unmatched convenience for local residents and families. We offer a curated selection of fashion for men, women, and children.

We are dedicated to supporting our community by partnering with local businesses and ensuring a friendly, welcoming shopping atmosphere for everyone.
